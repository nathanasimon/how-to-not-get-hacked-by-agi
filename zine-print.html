Why three factors? AI can clone voices. AI can intercept SMS codes. AI can trick phone verification. But AI cannot clone your fingerprint. AI cannot replicate your face. This is the final layer that stops AI attacks.

How voice cloning attacks work

1. Scrape your voice

AI finds 3 seconds of your voice from Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or any public video. Every video you post publicly is training data. AI doesn't need much.

2. Train the model

Commercial tools process your audio. Takes minutes. Costs $1/month. The AI learns your voice patterns, inflections, pauses. Everything that makes your voice yours.

3. Generate fake audio

AI can now say anything in your voice. "I need money." "I'm in trouble." "Verify your account." The cloned voice sounds real. Your family can't tell the difference. Neither can banks.

4. Execute the attack

Scammer calls your grandma. Calls your bank. Calls your boss. Uses your cloned voice. They ask for money. They ask for account access. They ask for passwords. It works.

How to protect yourself

AI can clone voices. It can bypass phone verification. It can trick authenticator apps. But AI cannot clone what you physically are or hold. Physical authentication is your strongest defense.

1

Get a physical security key.

YubiKey. Titan Security Key. These small devices plug into your computer or phone. They cannot be cloned. AI cannot replicate what you physically hold.

Physical keys are the strongest protection. AI cannot clone hardware.

2

Use biometric authentication everywhere.

Face ID. Fingerprint. Touch ID. Enable biometric login on your phone, computer, and apps. Your face and fingerprint cannot be cloned by AI.

Biometrics are physical. AI can clone voices but not your face or fingerprint.

3

Enable three-factor authentication.

Something you know (password) + something you have (smartphone) + something you are (fingerprint or face ID). This combination stops AI attacks completely.

Three factors: something you know + something you have + something you are. AI cannot clone your fingerprint or face.

4

Use authenticator apps with biometrics.

Google Authenticator. Authy. 1Password. Enable two-factor authentication everywhere. Use Face ID or fingerprint when possible. Never use SMS codes.

Authenticator apps with biometrics are stronger than SMS. AI can intercept phone calls but not your biometric.

5

Pick a family code word.

Choose a random phrase. Tell your family: if anyone calls asking for money, they must say this code word first.

AI can clone voices. It cannot know your secret.

6

Hang up and call back.

If anyone calls with an emergency, hang up. Call them back using a number you have saved. Do not use any number they provide.

Real emergencies can wait 2 minutes for verification.

7

Lock down social media voices.

Make your videos private. Remove voice samples from public posts. Scammers need 3 seconds of audio.

Less public audio means harder to clone.

8

Talk to your parents today.

Sit down with them. Explain voice cloning. Show them examples. Set up biometric authentication and the family code word together.

Elderly people lost $1 billion to AI scams in 2024.
