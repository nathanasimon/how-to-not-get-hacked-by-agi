How to protect yourself

AI can clone voices. It can trick phone verification. It can fool security apps. But AI cannot clone what you physically are or hold. Three actions to take today:

1
Enable three-factor authentication.
Something you know (password) + something you have (smartphone) + something you are (fingerprint or face ID). Use a physical security key like YubiKey when possible.
AI cannot clone your fingerprint or face. Physical keys cannot be replicated.
2
Pick a family code word.
Choose a random phrase. Tell your family: if anyone calls asking for money, they must say this code word first. Hang up and call back on a saved number.
AI can clone voices. It cannot know your secret.
3
Talk to your parents today.
Sit down with them. Explain voice cloning. Show them this zine. Set up biometric authentication and the family code word together.
Elderly people lost $1 billion to AI scams in 2024.
