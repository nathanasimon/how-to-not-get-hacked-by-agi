The SIM Swap Attack

Here's how it works:

1. AI clones your voice from TikTok videos

2. Hacker calls your phone company

3. They sound exactly like you

4. Company transfers your number to their phone

5. They get all your SMS codes

6. They're in. Everything.

Think of it like this:

Someone steals your house key AND convinces the locksmith you asked them to make a copy. That's SIM swapping with voice cloning.

"Two locks worked. Until AI learned how to pick the second one."

Two locks? Better. But not enough. Not anymore.
