The SIM Swap Attack

Here's how it works:

1
AI clones your voice from TikTok videos
2
Hacker calls your phone company
3
They sound exactly like you
4
Company transfers your number to their phone
5
They get all your SMS codes
6
They're in. Everything.
Think of it like this:

Someone steals your house key AND convinces the locksmith you asked them to make a copy. That's SIM swapping with voice cloning.

"Two locks worked. Until AI learned how to pick the second one."

Two locks? Better. But not enough. Not anymore.

3
Three Locks
SECURE

Password + Key + You

This is the answer.

For the AI age.

Something You Know

Your password

Something You Have

A YubiKey. A physical key you touch. Can't be hacked remotely. Can't be cloned.

Something You Are

Your fingerprint. Your face. AI can't fake your actual body.

Why This Works:

Even if AI steals your password. Even if they steal your phone number. They still need your fingerprint. They still need your physical key.

AI can't clone your body. AI can't hack a physical object in your pocket.

"Three locks. All strong. That's the only way to stay safe in the AI age."

What Happens When One Link Breaks

Weak password? AI guesses it. Chain broken.

SMS 2FA? SIM swap breaks it. Chain broken.

No biometric? They only need two things. Chain broken.

It's like a three-link chain:

If one link is weak, the whole chain breaks. If one lock is weak, your whole security breaks.

The Scary Combo

SIM Swap + Voice Cloning = Disaster

This is what happened to that $25 million company. Voice cloning got them past the phone company. SIM swap got them the codes. Weak password got them in.

Three weak links. One attack. Everything gone.

Three locks. All strong. That's the only way.

2. Enable 2FA (authenticator app, not SMS)

3. Use biometrics on your phone

Level Up

1. Get a YubiKey

2. Enable 3FA on important accounts

3. Protect your phone number with a PIN
